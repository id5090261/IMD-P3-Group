Description

This group was created on January 30, 2021 by Bani Rezaei to create a group with the aim of training programmers and teachers. Most of the members of the group are Iranian and from Iran, but in the future, with the expansion of this group, it will be transferred to other countries as well. In this group, tutorials related to hard skills and soft skills are discussed.
